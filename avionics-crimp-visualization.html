✈️ Avionics Wire Crimping

Per AC 43.13-1B Chapter 11

Proper Crimp Requirements

  • ✓ Strip length: 1/16" to 1/8"
  • ✓ No broken or nicked strands
  • ✓ Even barrel compression
  • ✓ Hex crimp pattern visible
  • ✓ Insulation support engaged
  • ✓ No protruding strands

Specifications

  • Wire: 22 AWG (7×30 strands)
  • Pin: High-density contact
  • Crimp Tool: M22520/2-01
  • Pull Test: Min 7.5 lbs

⚠ Always use calibrated crimp tools and verify with pull test
